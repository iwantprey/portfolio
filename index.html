Certifications & Compliance

As a professional security agency in the Philippines, our operations are built on a foundation of **strict compliance** and **official accreditation**. We are fully licensed and certified by all relevant government bodies, ensuring that our services meet the highest standards of safety, legality, and professionalism.

Our licenses and certifications include:

  • PNP-SOSIA License to Operate (LTO): We hold a valid License to Operate issued by the Philippine National Police - Supervisory Office for Security and Investigation Agencies (PNP-SOSIA). This is the fundamental license that legally authorizes a security agency to operate in the country, confirming our adherence to all national security laws and regulations.
    • LTO Number: PSA-2025-XX-YYYY
    • Expiration: December 31, 2026
  • Individual License to Exercise Security Profession (LESP): All our security personnel, from guards to supervisors, hold an individual LESP. This is a mandatory ID card issued by the PNP-SOSIA that certifies each officer has undergone the required training, background checks, and psychological examinations to legally practice their profession.
  • Firearms License & Permit to Carry: For armed security details, our officers are equipped with legally registered firearms and possess the necessary Permit to Carry Firearms Outside of Residence (PTCFOR), ensuring all operations are conducted with full legal authorization.
  • Philippine Association of Detective and Protective Agency Operators (PADPAO) Membership: We are a proud and active member of PADPAO, the leading industry organization in the Philippines. This membership signifies our commitment to ethical standards and best practices within the private security sector.

We undergo **continuous training** and re-certification to stay at the forefront of security standards, guaranteeing that our team remains the most reliable and trusted in the industry.
